LEONELL

A Multi-Family Cookbook

Welcome to Leonell!

This project was birthed from a love for food and wanting to create a convenience for the family.

My name is Matt and I have a family that loves to cook (and eat). My wife's family loves it so much that they created their own cookbook. However, we are now in the 21st century and most of us prefer to do things online, which is where this project comes in. This looks to hold all recipes from that cookbook, all recipes that I can get from my family, and have the capacity to add in new recipes when desired.
